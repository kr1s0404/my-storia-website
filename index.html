Every feature in Mystoria is grounded in research and designed to make a real difference in your child's daily life.

Personalized Stories

AI-powered stories tailored to your child's unique needs, routines, and comfort level.

Evidence-Based

Built on Carol Gray's Social Story methodology with proper sentence ratios and structure.

Daily Routines

Help your child prepare for everyday moments — from brushing teeth to visiting the doctor.

Visual & Engaging

Beautiful illustrations and a warm interface that children love to interact with.

Privacy First

Your child's data stays secure. On-device processing with COPPA-compliant design.

Family-Centered

Easy for parents and caregivers to create, customize, and share stories.

Simple for you.
Meaningful for them.

Creating a personalized social story takes just a few taps.

1

Describe the situation

Tell Mystoria about the routine or situation your child needs help with — a doctor visit, a new classroom, bedtime.

2

Personalize

Add your child's name, preferences, and comfort level. Mystoria adapts the language and detail to fit.

3

Read together

Share the story with your child before the event. Revisit it anytime to build familiarity and confidence.
